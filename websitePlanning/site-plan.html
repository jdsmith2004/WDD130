Brainstorm

Why will people come to your site?

The site will offer a unique experience allowing users to add and track goals right from the new tab screen. Additionaly it will display the weather and a personallized encouraging message as well as a quote. By using this people will be able to take control of their lives and start acomplishing their dreams.

What questions will your visitors expect your site to answer for them?

My website will not be designed to answer questions. I am building it because I know how hard it is for me to manage my time and want to develop a solution that others and I can both take advantage of to not only better oneself, but also have a visually appealing dashboard of exactly where and how much the improvements have taken place. When they come to the site, almost if not all of the users will have read the description on the chrome webstore describing the use case and purpose of my website. So when they first come it would be cool to have some sort of view where it gives the purpose in the middle of the screen and then some arrows pointing to different sections of the screen describing the purpose and use case for all of the buttons and widgets as well as where to go to make changes, for example the settings wheel would say "settings, go here for switching theme editing name, email, password, and so much more!" next to the arrow. Overall the goal of the website will Homely be to allow others to better themselves.

What types of activities will visitors want to complete on your site?

The answer for this question is pretty similar to the last one my website will be Homely productivy based and won't have any activities to participate in on the website. Some of the actions needed will be adding a name, email, and password. This will allow them to sign in, sync their data to the website and even download and sync their user profile across multiple browsers and computers.

What information is necessary to complete any actions your site offers?

No information will be required other than a name, email, and password as specified above.

Why will your visitors come to your site instead of another to get this information or complete the actions?

To my knowledge, I'm not aware of any competitors with any similar functions to my website. However, if by some miracle this idea ends up taking off and my website is succesfull my website will have to have advantages over the other websites in a similar doHome. In order to do that, now that I'm starting ahead I will have to stay ahead. I believe one of the best ways to develop and Hometain an advantage is crowd sourcing. I intend to add an idea suggestion/complaints spot somewhere in the website that will allow for idea suggestions. Next I'll make sure my website looks extremely clean and has minimal to no glitching as I push out updates. I'll have to have crisp animations that make the website feel alive and smooth color schemes and transitions as they check off their goals, evaluate their progress, etc.

How will users keep track of their goals, and their progress at completing them?

First off their will be a box at the bottom of the website with a row of spans 6 columns long that will put up a completion image after each goal is completed. Then my second page for the site will allow them to view their progress as a chart with the amount of total goals completed and also just the individual goals completed by percentage each month. I'm also considering adding a function where it shows a number in the middle of the checkbox that displays the days the goal has been checked off that week with an option to switch it to by month in settings. The other feature to be implemented will be a motivational message with their name at the end encouraging them to keep going and acopmlish their dreams.

Overview

Purpose

Have you every realized you were behind on your schedule and goals and wondered what happened? I pleased to tell you that I’m building a solution for that problem. I’d like to create an interactive chrome extension to fine tune your ability to manage your tasks and goal milestones. The extension will replace the new tab page and instead display the day, a motivational message, and in the top right a 2 by 3 grid filled with an abbreviation for your goals which can be checked off right there from the home tab. Additionally milestones and progress will be shown with the option to customize goals in settings.

Audience

The audience is primarily for students but will work and is recommended for everyone. By utilizing the site you will eventually be able to change your life and build strong productive habits you would not have otherwise. Because this website will be built to solve a common problem of goal and habit management there is no one we will not market to. After launch if we collect general user age info this will be subject to change
